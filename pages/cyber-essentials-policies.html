Cyber Essentials policies for SMEs: the minimum viable set

Most SMEs think they need “a policy library”. In practice, a few clear one-pagers (plus evidence screenshots) can remove days of back-and-forth with your MSP/assessor.

policies sme evidence

The 3 one-pagers to write

  1. Access control & leavers: how accounts are created, who approves admin, how leavers are removed (and how fast).
  2. Patching: what updates are automatic, what gets patched manually, and the SLA for critical updates.
  3. Device baseline: encryption, screen locks, AV, and how new devices are provisioned.

What to include (template prompts)

Keep it factual

Write what you do today, then improve it. Avoid aspirational policy statements you can’t evidence.

Name the owner

Each one-pager should name who owns the process (person or MSP) and where evidence lives.

List exceptions

If you have BYOD, shared devices, or site kiosks, document the boundary and mitigation.

Link to evidence

Include 2–3 bullets pointing to screenshots/exports that back it up.
