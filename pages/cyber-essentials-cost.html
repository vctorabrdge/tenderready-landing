Cyber Essentials cost (UK): what you’ll pay and what slows you down

If a tender requires Cyber Essentials, cost isn’t just the certification fee. The hidden cost is time: chasing evidence, rework with your MSP, and delays because blockers weren’t identified early.

cyber essentials certification cost tender deadlines hidden time costs

What affects Cyber Essentials cost

  • Company size and number of users/devices
  • Complexity: multiple sites, remote workers, mixed devices
  • How prepared you are (MFA, patching, admin controls)
  • Who helps: DIY vs MSP vs consultancy
  • Time pressure: tender deadlines often increase effort

The bit most SMEs underestimate: evidence

Why it costs time

Even if controls are implemented, you still need to prove it quickly: screenshots, exports, and short policy notes. Under deadline pressure, this becomes the bottleneck.

How to reduce it

Run a gap analysis early, assign each fix an owner, and collect evidence as you go — so your MSP/assessor gets a clean handoff pack, not a scavenger hunt.

DIY vs MSP: what to expect

DIY

  • Cheaper cash cost
  • More internal time
  • Works best with a structured checklist + evidence prompts

MSP / consultant assisted

  • Faster if they have access and clear scope
  • Costs rise if evidence is scattered / requirements unclear
  • Best when you hand them a clear gap report + evidence index
