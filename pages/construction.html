Cyber Essentials for construction & trades

If you’re bidding for work with councils, main contractors, or frameworks, Cyber Essentials becomes a gate. The problem isn’t the scheme — it’s getting ready fast, collecting evidence, and handing it to your MSP/assessor without endless back-and-forth.

construction tenders evidence pack

Typical reality in small firms

  • IT is “part-time” (office manager + MSP)
  • Evidence lives in WhatsApp, emails, and random screenshots
  • Tender deadlines compress everything into a few weeks

What wins

  • One owner per control, one due date per fix
  • Evidence captured as you go (not at the end)
  • A single handoff pack your MSP/assessor can act on immediately

What you actually need (in plain English)

  • MFA everywhere (especially admin accounts)
  • Patch visibility (prove updates are on and managed)
  • Device baseline (encryption + AV + screen locks)
  • Account hygiene (no shared logins; leavers removed fast)
  • Firewall ownership (who manages it, and proof of config)

If you can package the above with evidence, most “tender panic” disappears.
