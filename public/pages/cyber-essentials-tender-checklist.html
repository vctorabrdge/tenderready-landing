Cyber Essentials tender checklist: what to prepare before you bid

When a tender asks for Cyber Essentials, time is your enemy. This checklist is designed to get you to a credible “ready” position quickly — and package your evidence so your MSP/assessor can move fast.

tender checklist construction handoff pack

Before you start: write down these facts

  • How many staff and devices (approx.)
  • Cloud stack: Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace
  • Who manages the firewall/router (MSP?)
  • Any remote workers, site devices, or shared machines

The 10 evidence items that unblock most tenders

  1. MFA enforcement evidence (especially admins)
  2. User list + admin roles export
  3. Leavers process note
  4. Device encryption screenshots
  5. AV/Defender enabled screenshots
  6. Patching policy (1 page)
  7. Update settings screenshots / compliance proof
  8. Firewall/router ownership statement
  9. Firewall key settings screenshots (redacted)
  10. A short “blockers + ETA” gap report

What to send to your MSP/assessor

  • Your gap report (blockers + owners + due dates)
  • An evidence index (file list with notes)
  • Questions list (anything you’re unclear about)
