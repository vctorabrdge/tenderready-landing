Cyber Essentials firewall & gateway requirements (SME-friendly)

For many small firms, the firewall is “owned by the MSP”. That’s fine — but under tender deadlines you still need a simple evidence pack that proves it’s configured securely.

firewall internet gateway msp evidence

What you need (plain English)

  • No default admin passwords; admin access restricted.
  • Remote management is controlled and audited (or disabled).
  • Only necessary ports/services exposed to the internet.
  • Guest Wi‑Fi separated from company systems (where applicable).

Evidence checklist (what to screenshot / export)

If in-house

  • Make/model and firmware version
  • Admin access controls
  • Remote management settings
  • Port-forwarding / exposed services list

If MSP-managed

  • Short MSP statement: they manage the gateway and what they monitor
  • Screenshots of key settings (redacting sensitive data)
  • Confirmation of guest network separation (if used)

Construction/trades tip

If you bid for work with main contractors, you’ll be asked for evidence repeatedly. Store a “gateway evidence pack” once and re-use it rather than chasing your MSP every time.
