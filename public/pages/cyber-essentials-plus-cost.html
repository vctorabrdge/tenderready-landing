Cyber Essentials Plus cost: what changes vs Cyber Essentials and what to budget

Cyber Essentials Plus (CE+) adds verification and testing. The biggest driver of cost is still preparation: closing gaps and packaging evidence so testing doesn’t become weeks of back-and-forth.

cyber essentials plus cost ce+ evidence pack

What CE+ adds

  • Assessor-led verification (not just self-assessment)
  • Testing/sampling of devices and configurations
  • More emphasis on consistent implementation (not “one perfect laptop”)

Why CE+ costs more

  • More assessor time
  • Coordination overhead (device access, scheduling, evidence)
  • Remediation cycles if blockers show up late

How to keep CE+ cost under control

  1. Run a gap analysis first and identify the real blockers.
  2. Assign fixes with owners + deadlines (MSP vs internal).
  3. Collect evidence as you go so the assessor isn’t waiting on screenshots.
  4. Standardise device baselines (patching, AV, encryption, MFA) across the fleet.
