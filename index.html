The problem

  • Tasks aren’t owned ("someone needs to change that setting")
  • Evidence is scattered (screenshots in WhatsApp, docs in Drive)
  • Handoff to your MSP/assessor is messy (context lost → rework → delays)

The fix

  • Controls checklist mapped to the 5 Cyber Essentials areas
  • Tasks with owners + due dates
  • Evidence uploads attached to each control
  • Readiness score + blockers view
  • Exportable handoff pack (gap report + evidence index)

How it works

  1. Set up your environment (M365/Google, devices, remote working)
  2. Work through controls: assign tasks + upload evidence as you go
  3. Export a single handoff pack for your MSP/assessor

What’s in the handoff pack

Gap report

A simple, tender-friendly summary: what’s done, what’s missing, and what’s blocking readiness.

Evidence index

A control-by-control list of evidence items (file, date, note) so your assessor/MSP can move quickly.

Control notes

Plain-English notes per control: what you changed, where it lives, and who owns it.

Built for construction & trades: busy teams, tender deadlines, and MSP handoff — not a compliance textbook.

FAQ

Does this replace an MSP/assessor?

No. It makes the DIY work structured and makes handoff faster/cheaper.

Cyber Essentials vs Cyber Essentials Plus?

Built for CE readiness. Evidence hygiene and control ownership helps for CE+ as well.

Not in v1. We’ll add templates once the workflow is proven.
